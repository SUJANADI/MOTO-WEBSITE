About Motomanipal

MotoManipal, a student-led e-motorcycle racing team at Manipal Institute of Technology, excels in designing, manufacturing, and racing electric superbikes. Winners of the FMAE E-Bike Design Challenge and participants in MotoStudent India and the Asian E-Bike Challenge, they aim for MotoStudent International. Featured in Times of India, Deccan Herald, and Prajavani.

  • Our Mission

    We aim to redefine urban commuting with electric bikes that blend cutting-edge technology and sustainable design, making them a lifestyle choice.

  • Our Goal

    We aim to enhance urban mobility with green energy, pioneering stylish and eco-friendly electric bikes for sustainable transportation.
