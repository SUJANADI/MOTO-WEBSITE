MANAGEMENT



The management subsystem acts as a critical subsystem that coordinates various functions to ensure the team's success. It oversees the integration of technical aspects, such as motor performance and battery management, with logistical elements like race strategy, team communication, and resource allocation. Effective management ensures that engineers, riders, and support staff work cohesively, aligning their efforts towards achieving optimal performance on the track. By balancing innovation, efficiency, and teamwork, management helps navigate challenges, adapt to changing conditions, and ultimately drive the team towards victory in the highly competitive world of EV motorbike racing.
